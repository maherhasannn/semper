8-Week Program

The Curriculum

Each week builds on the last. By the end, you'll have a portfolio of AI projects and the skills to keep building.

Week 1
Intro to LLMs

Understand how large language models work under the hood. Explore the transformer architecture and see what makes models "large" vs "small."

Hands-on: Use Google Teachable Machine to see how changing weights affects model behavior. Jailbreak an LLM to understand context windows.

ChatGPT Claude Teachable Machine
Week 2
Context Engineering

Build a persistent AI personality that remembers you. Learn how to use project knowledge bases to upload writing style, past projects, and interests.

Project: Create a College Counselor Agent with knowledge of your top 5 target colleges built into its memory.

Claude Projects Knowledge Bases Custom Instructions
Week 3
Multi-Modal Problem Solving

Dive into vision models - how they work and why they sometimes break. Learn to leverage AI for creative and technical tasks.

Project: Draw a website frontend on paper, photograph it, and have Claude write the code for it.

Vision Models Claude Image-to-Code
Week 4
AI Automation

Learn to orchestrate AI to take multiple steps without being asked twice. Understand "if this, then that" logic at a high level.

Project: Build an agent that finds trending topics in a specific niche and writes tweets automatically.

Zapier Automation AI Agents
Week 5
Coding Without Syntax

AI breaks down the walls of traditional coding. Learn how to describe what you want and let AI handle the syntax.

Project: Build and deploy your own "Link in Bio" website from scratch using AI coding assistants.

Replit Agent Cursor Deployment
Week 6
Deepfakes & Safety

It's incredibly easy to make a lifelike digital clone of someone. We'll prove it by doing exactly that - then learn how to spot fakes.

Project: Create a deepfake of yourself, then analyze the forensic markers that prove it's fake.

ElevenLabs Midjourney Content Credentials
Week 7
Data Science

Feed large datasets into an LLM to find trends. Use real data to identify ways to improve your neighborhood or school.

Project: Develop a unique proposal to improve your community based on actual data analysis.

Data Analysis Pandas NumPy
Week 8
Demo Day

Present your final project to the class. Based on your Week 7 proposal, build and demonstrate a solution using multiple AI tools.

Requirement: Your solution must incorporate at least 2 different AI tools working together.

Presentation Portfolio Project Multi-Tool Integration

FAQs

Common questions about the AI Foundations course.

Do I need coding experience?

No prior coding experience is required. Week 5 specifically covers "Coding Without Syntax" - using AI to build without needing to know programming languages. That said, if you want to go deeper into the engineering side, we can arrange that separately.

What's included in the college counseling?

In Week 2, every student builds a personalized College Counselor Agent with deep knowledge of their top 5 target schools. This agent remembers your writing style, interests, and past projects. It's a tool you'll keep using long after the course ends.

What age group is this for?

The course is designed for high school students, but the content is college-level. We focus on practical applications that prepare students for both higher education and the real world.

Is the deepfake lesson ethical?

Absolutely. We teach students to create deepfakes of themselves specifically to understand how they work and how to detect them. Understanding the technology is the first step to protecting against misuse. We cover content credentials, forensic markers, and the safeguards that exist.

What do I need to participate?

A laptop and internet connection. Some weeks require specific setup (Week 3 for the vision model project), but we'll walk through everything. Most tools we use have free tiers or educational access.
