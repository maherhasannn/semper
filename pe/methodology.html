How we work

Methodology

Our approach is systematic, continuous, and deliberately quiet. We surface private market opportunities by reading structure where others see noise.

Data Ingestion

We maintain direct integrations with 147 independent data feeds spanning public registries, regulatory filings, and licensed commercial providers across North America and Europe. Every source is validated for reliability and cross-referenced against at least two others before entering our pipeline.

We do not scrape. All data is obtained through legitimate channels with proper licensing agreements in place.

Entity Resolution

Private companies exist across multiple registries, filings, and databases under varying names, structures, and identifiers. Our entity resolution layer reconciles these fragments into unified company profiles, maintaining a living graph of 2.4 million entities.

The graph is fully reconciled every 24 hours, with critical signals processed in near real-time.

Signal Detection

Not all changes are meaningful. We distinguish signal from noise by analyzing patterns that historically correlate with actionable opportunities:

  • Ownership structure changes and succession indicators
  • Financial trajectory inflections (growth acceleration, margin expansion)
  • Regulatory filings suggesting strategic transitions
  • Management team changes and hiring patterns
  • Competitive positioning shifts within sector clusters

Each signal is scored against our proprietary model, with only high-confidence opportunities surfaced to clients.

Validation

Before any opportunity reaches a client dashboard, it passes through a multi-stage validation process:

  • Automated cross-reference against three or more independent sources
  • Consistency checks against historical entity behavior
  • Anomaly detection to flag potential data quality issues
  • Final review against current market conditions

Delivery

Validated opportunities are delivered through secure client dashboards with full provenance tracking. Every data point can be traced back to its source, ensuring complete transparency and auditability.

Our methodology is continuously refined based on client feedback and market evolution. We publish quarterly methodology updates to all active clients.
